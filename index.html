About Us

At Code Imaging Center, we are dedicated to advancing the field of radiology with cutting-edge imaging technology and unparalleled expertise. Our team of experienced radiologists and medical professionals is committed to delivering precise diagnostic imaging that enhances patient care and supports accurate diagnoses. With a focus on innovation and excellence, we utilize the latest advancements in medical imaging to provide reliable and insightful results. Our mission is to ensure every patient receives top-quality care and to continuously improve the standards of radiology imaging.

Our Services

Diagnostic X-ray

Accurate imaging for quick and effective diagnosis.

Mammography

Advanced breast imaging for early detection and prevention.

Ultrasound

Real-time imaging for comprehensive diagnostic insights.

Computed Tomography (CT-Scan)

High-resolution cross-sectional images for detailed analysis.

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

In-depth imaging with superior clarity and precision.

Nuclear Medicine

Innovative imaging to evaluate bodily functions and detect diseases.

Bone Density Scan (DEXA)

Accurate assessment of bone health and osteoporosis risk.

Fluoroscopy

Dynamic imaging to guide real-time diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.

Positron Emission Tomography (PET/CT)

Advanced imaging for metabolic activity and disease detection.
